# About

I'm Kade — a software engineer with a background in AI and machine learning.

## Background

<!-- Fill in your background — where you've worked, studied, or built things -->
I did my undergrad in Annapolis, where I studied computer science and computer engineering. My senior year, I was fortunate to win a Marshall Scholarship, which allowed me to pursue masters degrees in AI and machine learning at the University of Sussex and then the University of Cambridge. 

During my time in Brighton, I focused on neuroevolution and spiking neural networks which I continued to explore as part of an interest in quantization and efficient deep learning. After moving to Cambridge, I started working on AI safety and security, connecting with the UK AI Security Institute and writing a thesis on the outlook of foundation models in cybersecurity as well as potential countermeasures against AI agents conducting cyber attacks.

## Interests

- **Technical**: AI safety and security, ML systems
- **Outside of code**: Travel and hiking
